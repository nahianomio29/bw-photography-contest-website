Rules and Regulations

The rules are straighforward. The categories and requirements are be listed below.

  • Deadline: The last date to submit your work is 16 December, 2024. After the date no submission will be taken into consideration.
  • Requirement: Each participant is allowed to submit at most 3 photographs, one from each category listed below.
  • Photograph Requirement: Photography can be either landscape or potrait but in black-and-white theme. All photograph needs to be original content, any copied photography will be automatically disqualified and participant will be eliminated from the contest.
  • Voting Rules: Voting should be limited to one vote per person only. Any additional votes will be denied, only the first vote will be taken into consideration.
  • Content Guidelines: All content should be free from nudity, racial discrimination, and controversial topics.


Category

This year, the contest will be held based on three categories of photography.

  1. Vehicles: Own a vehicle you are proud of or a come just caught your eyes in the middle of a walk? Want to showoff your great vehicle photography skills? Post the pictures here to get a chance to win and be featured in next year's gallery
  2. Bridges and Waterfall: Went on a trip where the attraction was the waterfall? A beautiful bridge was on your way to the trip? Show your skills by submitting the photo you took of the bridge or the waterfall.
  3. Building: Do you live in a big city? Or did you take a trip to a downtown and walked on the streets surrounding massive buldings? Show us what caught your eyes and your skills of city photography.


Thank your for taking the time to read the rules and regulations and we hope that the rules will be followed by all participants. If you have any question, you can reach us or submit your inquiries here.
