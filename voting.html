Vote for your Favorite Photograph

We thank you for taking the time and go through the nominated photography and pick your favorite. There will be three winners, one from each category with the highest vote. Remember, you can only vote for one photo per category. You will not have the option to select more than one from each category. If a photo is unclear or distorted, you can click on the photo itself to view it in its full size and resolution which can help you pick your favorites. Thank you for the opportunity to participate in the selection process. We'll carefully review each nominated photograph in order to make a thoughtful decision for each category.

The competition is between photography for three categories: Vehicle, Bridges & Waterfalls, and Buildings. Any photography not meeting the category requirements were eliminated in the beginning. After careful consideration and preliminary voting two photos were selected for the final round. Be sure to vote for your favorites as your votes will determine the winners for the 2024 Black-and-White photography contest. As a participant in the judging process, I understand the importance of selecting the most deserving photographs for each category. I'll carefully examine the finalists in the Vehicle, Bridges & Waterfalls, and Buildings categories, considering their composition, creativity, and emotional impact. Each category offers a unique perspective, challenging me to appreciate the beauty captured through the lens of talented photographers.
