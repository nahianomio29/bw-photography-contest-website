Submit your Photography for 2024

Here you can participate in the Black-and-White Photography contest by submitting your photographs for each category. The categories for this year are Vehicles, Bridges & Waterfalls, and Buildings. You can submit only one photograph from each category.

The rules are simple, the photographs need to be in image format such as JPG, JPEG, RAW, PNG, and GIF. The file size can be any, but we recommend that it does not exceed over 15 megabytes. The deadline for submission of photographs is 12/16/2024. Any submission after the deadline will not be taken into consideration.

Also, follow the Rules and Regulations page for more information.

In addition to submitting the photographs, fill out the form at the bottom with your full name, contact number, and email address. Be sure to include a description of the photographs in the text box.
